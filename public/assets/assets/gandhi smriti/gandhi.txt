A Place of Reflection & Inspiration
Gandhi Smriti, formerly known as Birla House, is one of the most significant memorials dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi. Located on Tees January Road, Delhi, it is the place where Gandhi spent the last 144 days of his life before his assassination on January 30, 1948.

Now converted into a museum and memorial, it stands as a tribute to his life, principles, and immense contribution to India's independence. It offers visitors a deep and emotional connection to the Father of the Nation, making it a must-visit for history lovers and those seeking inspiration.
